# Our Approach

We're a calm, welfare-first salon. Here's how that shows up in practice.

## No rushing, ever.

Every dog gets the time they need. We don't double-book or hurry through appointments.

## We respect medical needs.

Arthritis, skin conditions, post-surgery — we adapt our techniques and products accordingly.

## We don't force styles you don't want.

This is your dog. We listen to what you want, ask questions if we're unsure, and never assume.

## We'll tell you if something needs attention.

Lumps, ear infections, dental issues — if we spot something, we'll let you know. Sometimes dogs need a vet, not just another groom.

## Nervous dogs are welcome.

We've worked with anxious, reactive, and elderly dogs for over 40 years. Patience isn't an extra — it's how we work.
